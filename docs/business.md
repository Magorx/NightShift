# Night Shift -- Business Tracker

## Market Position

- **Genre**: Factory roguelite with defense mechanics
- **Closest comp**: Tower Factory (20K-50K copies, 88% positive, ~$330K-$825K gross)
- **Differentiators**: Elemental resource system, factory-IS-defense (not separate turrets), psychedelic aesthetic, scarred-factory narrative, player character
- **Target price**: $6.99-$7.99

## Revenue Projections

| Scenario | Copies Y1 | Gross Revenue | Net (after Steam 30%) |
|----------|-----------|---------------|----------------------|
| Pessimistic | 20K-30K | $140K-$240K | $98K-$168K |
| Conservative | 50K-100K | $350K-$800K | $245K-$560K |
| Optimistic | 150K-300K | $1M-$2.4M | $735K-$1.68M |
| Breakout | 500K+ | $3.5M+ | $2.45M+ |

*Conservative = Tower Factory tier but with stronger identity. Optimistic = Dome Keeper / Thronefall tier. Breakout = viral moment (Lethal Company effect).*
